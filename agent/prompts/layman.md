Explain the current topic in plain language for a non-technical audience.

Requirements:
- Be concise and easy to scan
- Do not use analogies
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Focus on meaning, impact, purpose, and outcome
- Only include technical details if they are necessary for clarity
- Adapt to the context whether this is a problem, decision, change, proposal, status update, process, or general explanation
